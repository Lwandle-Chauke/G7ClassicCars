About G7 Classic Cars

G7 Classic cars is a premier vintage and classic car rental service that brings the elegance and charm of motoring history to life. Based in Johannesburg, we specialise in the rental of impeccably maintained vintage vehicles for weddings, matric dances, special events, photoshoots, and film productions. Whether you’re looking to make a grand entrance or simply relive the golden age of motoring, we offer timeless rides for unforgettable moments.
